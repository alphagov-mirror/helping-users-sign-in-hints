To set up a GOV.UK Verify account, you need:

  • at least one photo identity document (ID)
  • a UK address
  • a mobile phone

You can use a valid passport, full or provisional driving licence, or non-UK ID card.

If you’re just using one of those, you’ll also need a phone that can download an app and take pictures.


Other identity documents

For security, you can’t use:

  • any other identity documents (like railcards or library cards)
  • a paper-only driving licence
  • expired documents - except passports that ran out in the last 3 months

You can use passports and driving licenses from any country.

If you don’t have any valid documents, see how to continue without using GOV.UK Verify.
