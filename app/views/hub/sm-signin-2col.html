What is GOV.UK Verify?

GOV.UK Verify is designed by the UK government to fight the growing problem of identity theft.

Identity providers apply to be part of GOV.UK Verify. They are certified when they meet security and privacy standards set by government so you can trust them with your personal information.

When a government service uses GOV.UK Verify, you can access the service with the digital identity you set up with the identity provider.
