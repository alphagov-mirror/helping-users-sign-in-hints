You might not be able to have your identity verified

To verify an identity, certified companies access credit records then ask you to confirm this information. Most young people and newcomers to the UK have a limited ‘credit history’, companies might not be able to check your identity in this way.

What you’ll need on your credit record

Your identity might be verified if you have 2 or more of the following:

  • current account with a UK bank
  • credit card
  • personal loan
  • mortgage
  • gas or electricity bills
  • mobile phone contract
  • you’re registered to vote

You’ll also need a UK passport or a UK driving licence.
