Where to find your Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR)

Your UTR is on previous tax returns and other documents from HMRC, for example:

  • notices to file a return
  • payment reminders

You can also find your UTR in your HMRC online account.

If you can’t find your UTR call the Self Assessment helpline if you need your UTR and can’t find any documents from HMRC.
