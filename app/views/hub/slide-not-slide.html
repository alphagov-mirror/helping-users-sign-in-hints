Setting up an online identity

You'll need to set up your own online identity so you can safely and securely {{serviceNameLower}}.

The setting up your online identity takes 4 steps:

  • Pick which online identity you want to create

    You have a choice of online identities that are certified to sign you in to Government services, it's your choice you you sign up with.

  • Create your online identity with the provider

    You'll create your online identity which you can then use every time you sign in to number of Government services.

  • Confirm your idenity

    You'll need to provide some documents that will be double checked with the agency that provided them to you.

  • Continue to your service

    You can then access the service safely and securely when we are sure that you are you you say you are.

You'll need about 15 minutes to get set up.
